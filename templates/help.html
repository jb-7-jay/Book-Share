Donor's Guide

What kind of books can i share ?

We accept following kinds of books

  • Engineering Text books
  • Medical Text books
  • reference books

What kinds of books I cannot share with SharedShelf ?

We don't accept these following books

  • Magazines
  • Periodicals
  • Xerox or Photocopied or Spirel bound study material even if they are in very good conditions.
